3a. Steps to Follow for a Legal Strike

‚öñÔ∏è Legal Process Requirements
üìã CCMA Procedures

Required Legal Steps:

  1. Internal Resolution: Attempt to resolve dispute with Department of Education
  2. CCMA Referral: Refer the dispute to CCMA or relevant bargaining council
  3. Conciliation Process: CCMA must attempt resolution within 30 days
  4. Certificate Issued: CCMA issues certificate of non-resolution if unsuccessful
  5. Strike Notice: Union must provide 48 hours written notice of strike action
  6. Protected Status: Strike must relate to matters of mutual interest (pay, conditions)
