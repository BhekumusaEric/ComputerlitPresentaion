üìã CCMA PROCESS

Legal Steps Required:

  1. Internal Resolution: Attempt to resolve with Department of Education
  2. CCMA Referral: Refer dispute to CCMA or relevant bargaining council
  3. Conciliation: CCMA tries to resolve within 30 days
  4. Certificate: CCMA issues certificate of non-resolution
  5. Notice: Union gives 48 hours written notice
  6. Protected Strike: Only for matters of mutual interest
